Kristina Randrup | Cartographer & GIS Analyst & Much More

To start with the basics, I have a BS in Environmental Science and Resource Management, with a concentration in Wildlife Management, which I earned at the University of Washington. At the same time, I earned minors in both Math and Quantitative Science. I am on my way towards a Masters in GIS with the University of Wisconsin.

Between undergraduate and graduate programs, I worked as a GIS Intern with The Nature Conservancy for almost a year, primarily studying Natural Climate Solutions.

Outside of work and school, I run, run, and run. When I'm not working hard at a computer, I'm working hard on a trail running up a mountain or racing farther than a marathon. Someday I'll hopefully be running on a world team competing on a big stage with many top notch athletes.
